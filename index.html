Bryon Kucharski

I am a second year MS student in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst graduating in May of 2020. Currently looking for full-time positions as a research engineer, machine learning engineer, or data scientist. I am currently completing a MS Project advised by Dr. Marc-Alexandre Côté and Trapit Bansal. I am working on improving generalization by leveraging knowledge graphs for reinforcement learning in text-based environments. In the spring of 2019, I spent the semester researching deep learning methods for multi-resolution time series data in the Information Fusion Laboratory under Dr. Madalina Fiterau. Before UMass, I earned a BS in Computer Engineering from Wentworth Institute of Technology and conducted research under Dr. Aaron Carpenter and Dr. Mehmet Ergezer.
